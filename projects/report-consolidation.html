Enterprise Report Consolidation (31 → 1)

SQL ETL Data Pipeline Automation

Diagnosed and fixed critical fiscal year-end failures caused by hardcoded date logic, then consolidated 31 separate reporting jobs into a single automated process — eliminating 40+ hours of monthly maintenance.

Problem

31 separate reporting jobs with hardcoded date logic failed every fiscal year-end, impacting multiple business stakeholders across 5 product subcategories. Manual maintenance consumed 40+ hours monthly across BI and business teams.

Approach

Identified the root cause (hardcoded fiscal year boundaries), then made the architectural decision to consolidate rather than patch. Implemented dynamic date logic, automated previously manual calculations, and built a centralized delivery platform with stakeholder-specific filtering.

Technical Details

  • Consolidated 31 separate ETL jobs into a single automated process
  • Dynamic date logic that automatically adjusts to fiscal year boundaries
  • Automated financial calculations replacing manual extraction
  • Multi-timeframe analysis (weekly, monthly, quarterly, annual)
  • Centralized delivery platform with stakeholder-specific filtering

Impact

  • 97% reduction in job count (31 → 1)
  • 40+ hours monthly maintenance eliminated
  • Dynamic date logic eliminates fiscal year-end failures
  • Centralized platform serves 5 subcategory teams through a single interface
