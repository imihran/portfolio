Liquidation Analytics Platform & Catalog Defect Viewer

SQL BI Dashboards Data Pipeline Monitoring ETL

Built the central hub for liquidation data reporting — a multi-tab dashboard tracking 7 liquidation types with volume-adjusted metrics, plus a proactive catalog defect viewer that identifies issues before they cause financial losses.

Problem

No self-service tooling existed for liquidation analysis. Teams relied on custom ad-hoc queries, and catalog defects (incorrect expiration dates, shelf life settings) caused premature liquidations that went undetected until financial impact was realized.

Approach

Built a comprehensive analytics platform with 5 dashboard tabs covering multiple business scopes and 7 liquidation types. Added a proactive Catalog Defect Viewer that monitors 6 expiration-related attributes with week-over-week trend snapshots and financial impact context, enabling teams to resolve issues before they cause losses.

Technical Details

  • 5 dashboard tabs with multi-level drill-down analysis
  • 7 liquidation types: Expired, Carrier Damaged, Customer Damaged, Warehouse Damaged, Vendor Damaged, Defective, Overstock
  • Volume-adjusted metrics (cost per shipped unit, cost as % of product cost) for rate-based trend evaluation
  • Catalog Defect Viewer monitoring 6 expiration attributes with WoW trend analysis
  • Drill-down to top 100 item-location-vendor combinations with raw data export
  • Optimized underlying data job for performance

Impact

  • Central hub for all liquidation reporting — eliminated need for custom ad-hoc queries
  • Proactive catalog defect identification prevents premature liquidations
  • Week-over-week defect trend snapshots with financial context
  • Serves Product Management, Category teams, and operational workstreams
