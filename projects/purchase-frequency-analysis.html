Purchase Frequency & Cross-Sell Analysis

SQL Data Analysis Business Strategy Revenue Modeling

Deep analysis of customer purchase patterns and product relationships in a $2.3B category that revealed a $265M+ revenue opportunity and directly drove product feature development.

Problem

Category teams lacked empirical understanding of how customers purchase related products together (e.g., machines and consumables). No framework existed to quantify cross-sell opportunities or identify high-value prospects.

Approach

Built SQL frameworks for purchase frequency analysis and attach rate modeling between durable goods and consumable products. Quantified category-level revenue opportunities and identified the highest-value prospect despite low current attach rates. Scaled the methodology beyond the initial category to other product combinations.

Technical Details

  • Customer purchase frequency analysis SQL frameworks
  • Attach rate modeling between durable goods and consumable products
  • Category-level revenue opportunity quantification
  • Scalable methodology documentation for cross-category application

Impact

  • Revealed $265M+ revenue opportunity
  • Analysis directly informed VP-level prioritization document
  • Product features shipped: post-add-to-cart attach widgets on mobile and desktop
  • Methodology scaled beyond initial category to other product combinations
