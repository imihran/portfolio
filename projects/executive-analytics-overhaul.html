Executive Vendor Analytics Platform

SQL Data Modeling Process Design Documentation

Led the system redesign for executive-level vendor meetings with top 30 strategic partners, establishing formalized intake processes and standardized scorecards that reduced preparation time from 3-5 days to under 1 hour.

Problem

Executive vendor meetings relied on fragmented, ad-hoc reporting with inconsistent metric definitions. Each scorecard took 3-5 days of manual preparation, and stakeholders frequently disagreed on metric interpretations.

Approach

Redesigned the entire reporting workflow: created a centralized wiki with documentation and DIY sections, built standardized scorecard templates, and implemented a formalized intake process requiring business context and benchmarking requirements before metric development begins.

Technical Details

  • Standardized scorecard templates with consistent metric definitions across all vendor meetings
  • Formalized intake workflow with business justification requirements
  • Self-service documentation enabling stakeholders to produce their own reports
  • Metric definition alignment across multiple business functions

Impact

  • Reduced scorecard preparation from 3-5 days to under 1 hour
  • 15+ successful executive meetings delivered
  • Standards adopted by 3 sister teams across the organization
  • Eliminated recurring disagreements on metric definitions
