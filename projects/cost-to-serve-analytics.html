Cost to Serve Analytics Framework

SQL Data Analysis Cost Modeling Business Strategy

Led BI development for an SVP-sponsored initiative delivering the first vendor Cost to Serve analysis, establishing a replicable framework for transparent cost-based vendor negotiations.

Problem

Vendor negotiations lacked transparency into actual cost structures. No framework existed to quantify packaging costs, inventory holding costs, or network transfer costs at the vendor level.

Approach

Designed analytics spanning three workstreams: (1) Packaging Analysis — quantifying prep unit volumes to identify cost reduction opportunities, (2) Forecast Metrics — comparing pre/post enrollment inventory levels and fulfillment costs, and (3) Network Methodology — estimating inter-facility transfer costs to surface efficiency opportunities.

Technical Details

  • Packaging analysis pipeline quantifying prep unit volumes by vendor catalog
  • Forecast metrics comparing pre/post enrollment inventory and fulfillment costs
  • Network cost estimation methodology for inter-facility transfers
  • Vendor-facing presentation framework balancing transparency with competitive sensitivity

Impact

  • First Cost to Serve analysis delivered for a top strategic partner
  • Established replicable framework for top vendor rollout
  • Enables data-driven vendor negotiations with transparent cost structures
  • Will influence executive conversations with 30+ strategic vendors
